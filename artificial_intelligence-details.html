Course Overview

This 2-month Artificial Intelligence (AI) course introduces learners to the foundations of AI, focusing on building intelligent systems capable of decision-making, learning, and problem-solving. It combines theoretical concepts with practical applications, ensuring students gain real-world experience in applying AI technologies.

  • Core Concepts: Learn supervised and unsupervised learning, data preprocessing, and model evaluation.

  • Hands-on Practice: Implement algorithms using Python and libraries like TensorFlow and scikit-learn.

  • Focus: Develop end-to-end machine learning projects with real-world datasets to build practical expertise.
